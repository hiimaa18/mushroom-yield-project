# Linear Regression Coefficient Interpretation

Temperature shows a positive coefficient, suggesting higher temperatures are associated with increased mushroom yield when other variables remain fixed.

Humidity shows a positive coefficient, indicating that higher humidity may support mushroom production.

CO₂ shows a negative coefficient, suggesting higher CO₂ levels are associated with reduced yield in this dataset.

The temperature-humidity interaction feature contributes additional information regarding combined environmental effects.

Because features were scaled using MinMaxScaler, coefficients represent relative influence rather than direct unit changes.
